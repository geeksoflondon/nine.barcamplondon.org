Essential Information

When

10am (BST) Saturday October 29th 2011 -- 4.45pm (GMT) Sunday 30th October 2011.

Where

Social Sciences Building, City University, St. John Street, London.

Travel

You'll find details of various ways of getting to BarCamp London 9 below. For up-to-date travel information, make sure to check the TfL web site.

Mainline and Overground

  • Farringdon (First Capital Bedford – Kent (Brighton, Sutton)) – 12 mins walk (Walking Directions)
  • Kings Cross St Pancras – 24 mins walk / or 23 mins with bus (Take the Route Bus 4 from Stop: US towards Archway)
  • Old Street – 16 mins walk

Nearest Tubes

  • Angel (Northen Line, Bank Branch) – 10 mins walk (Walking Directions)
  • Old Street (Northen Line, Bank Branch) – 16 mins walk
  • Farringdon Closed this weekend for major upgrade works

  • 56 – from Whipps Cross Roundabout towards St. Bartholomew’s Hospital

Cycling from Waterloo

  • Come out of Waterloo Station, cross York Road and pass under flyover to back of Royal Festival Hall
  • Turn right, enjoy a few minutes of easy cycling passing the back of the National Theatre, ITN and Gabriels Wharf.
  • At the end, turn left onto and cross Blackfriars Bridge.
  • Continue north, onto Farringdon Road.
  • After a few minutes, turn right onto Exmouth Market (Exmouth Street) at Roseberry Avenue cross roads
  • Continue straight, passing through bike road break to the Social Sciences building.

Car Parking

Here are some car parks local to the venue. See Parkopedia for more.

Northampton Square

Parking in the roads surrounding the Northampton Square site is at parking
meters. The nearest National Car Park to Northampton Square is on Skinner
Street, off St John Street.

Bunhill Row

Parking in the roads surrounding Bunhill Row is at parking meters. The nearest
National Car Parks to Cass Business School are at Finsbury Square, Tabernacle
Street, Clere Street and Paul Street.

West Smithfield

Parking in the roads surrounding West Smithfield is at parking meters. The
nearest National Car Parks to West Smithfiield are at Smithfield Central
Market, Snowhill and Cowcross Street.

Inns of Court School of Law

Parking in the roads surrounding the Inns of Court School of Law is at parking
meters. The nearest National Car Parks to the Inns of Court School of Law are
Selkirk House, Museum Street or International Press Centre, Shoe Lane.
